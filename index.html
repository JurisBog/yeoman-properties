Who are we?

We are a private registered landlord with 3 HMO licensed properties in the centre of town. We have been letting to students for over 15 years and have a sound knowledge and experience of dealing with them. Our properties are regarded as some of the best student properties in the town, they are of a high standard, well equipped and maintained properly. We are also regarded as good and fair landlords who look after our tenants properly. Quality, safety and fairness are some of our main objectives. Our lease terms are for 11 months (48 weeks) September to August. Town and Gown in St Andrews interview our prospective tenants and do the selection on our behalf. We are looking for mature and respectful students who will look after our properties. Our leases are very strict on anti social behaviour and parties of any shape or form are not allowed, so if you are looking for a party time then we are not the landlord for you.
